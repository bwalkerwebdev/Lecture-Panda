Virtual Expos Made Easy

What We Provide

An Engaging Experience

Participants will enjoy browsing your expo due to its familiar layout and playful interface. Proudly display the booths in your virtual expo in a customizable layout. Categorize booths by topic to make them easier to find. Promote your partners by giving them a featured slot.

Intimate Conversations

When there are more than just a few people in a video conference, participants start to clam up and genuine conversation breaks down. Your exhibitors can add up to 25 small group meeting rooms to their booth to facilitate open discussion and genuine interaction.

Give Your Exhibitors Control

Stop emailing back and forth with your exhibitors. After you create their booth, invite them to manage their own content. Exhibitors can upload files, embed websites or add custom content. Stop the flurry of emails with last minute changes.
